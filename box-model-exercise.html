Let's Make PB & J

Instructions:

  1. Gather Your Things
    • Plate
    • Bread
    • Peanut Butter Container
    • Jelly Container
    • Utensil
  2. Pull Out Two Slices of Bread
  3. Apply Peanut Butter to the First Slice Using the Utensil
  4. Apply Jelly to the Other Slice
  5. Put the Slices together with the PB and Jelly sides facing towards each other
  6. Put Away Your Stuff
    • Bread
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jelly
    • Utensil: in the sink
  7. mmmmm.....PBnJ

It Should Look Something Like This:

Bread
Jelly
Peanut Butter
Bread
Plate
